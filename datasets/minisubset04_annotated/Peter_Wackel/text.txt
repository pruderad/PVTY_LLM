Peter Wackel (born Steffen Peter Haas on 4 January 1977 in Erlangen) is a German singer.

Life
Wackel sings German Schlager songs. He is very popular in Mallorca. Wackel is also a big hit in the German city of Düsseldorf and is often played in their biggest night time hot spot Anton's.Frankfurter Rundschau: Schlagernacht, Vorfeiern für den Sommer (german)
