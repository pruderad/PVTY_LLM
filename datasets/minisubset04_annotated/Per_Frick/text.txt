Per Samuel Frick (born 14 April 1992) is a Swedish profession footballer who plays for IF Elfsborg in Allsvenskan as a striker. He has won two caps for the Sweden national team, scoring one goal. He is one of the co-founders of the Swedish clothing brand Ciszere.
