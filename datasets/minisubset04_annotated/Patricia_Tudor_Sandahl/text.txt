Patricia Ann Tudor Sandahl, née Howard, also Rosén from previous marriageSveriges befolkning 1970, CD-ROM, Version 1.04, Sveriges Släktforskarförbund (2002). (born 19 July 1940 in Manchester, England) is a Swedish psychotherapist and author. She is a doctor in philosophy and pedagogics, and a philosophy licentiat in sociology, psychologist. Tudor Sandahl is a retreat-leader for the Stiftelsen Berget in Rättvik, Sweden.

In 1981, she married professor Christer Sandahl.
